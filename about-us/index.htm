Know About Us

softecK is a certified website and application development company established in year 2016.

We are digital natives with a pioneering approach to Ecommerce solutions, web development, Web designing , software development, Software Consulting, Digital Marketing, Mobile App Development, Graphic designing and rest IT solutions.

At softecK, we guarantee the success with our efforts, hardwork, Customer centric approach and organized team efforts.

The USP of softecK lies in managing the project as we believe in keeping the transparency in our work. With this is mind, we engage our client throughout the lifecycle of the project and take their suggestions, feedbacks in each phase of project like designing, developing that helps us in developing and delivering best user experience to our end clients and end users.
